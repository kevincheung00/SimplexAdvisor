Meet Some Beats.

Start a Silent Disco.

Explore the World.

Using Spotify and Google Maps APIs, Meet Your Beat enables users to explore the world and find Spotify songs.

Users have the ability to "drop a beat", a song from Spotify that acts as a live beacon for other users to listen to.

By connecting to your Spotify account, you can play the song at any beacon. Anytime. Anywhere.

Whether you want to start a silent disco with others, share your favourite song to the world, or find new music,
Meet Your Beat lets you explore the world through music.
